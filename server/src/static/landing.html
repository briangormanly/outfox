Introduction

Manage, track, and achieve your learning goals with Outfox LMS. Group and share resources with friends. No cost, no expiration, a free lifetime of learning. Outfox is an enterprise LMS. Power up your learning with the features and integrations you need. We're known for our customer support. Quick responses, 24/7. We care about solving your training challenges. Outfox LMS has been built with the user in mind. We obsess over making workflows as easy as they are powerful. Outfox is 100% open source. No need to take out your wallet. We got you covered.

Background

Outfox is a Marist College capping project whose production began in the Fall of 2020. Every year since then, it has been passed down and perfected by students of following classes.

Mission

Here at Outfox, we believe that learning is not just about teachers and students. This is why Outfox allows you to bring everyone together, from students, to teachers, coaches, parents, and administrators using just one communication and collaboration platform.

Outfox is meant to be a free, open source, learning tool for students and teachers. It grants students the ability to connect with their professors and colleages seemlessly.
